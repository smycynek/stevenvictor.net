Baby Shape Swipe

About

Baby Shape Swipe is a geometry flash card app for the iPhone/iPad that parents can use with young children just learning to talk and recognize shapes. Just swipe, tap, or shake to explore and start a conversation about shapes and colors with your child.

A perfect activity for while waiting for a doctor appointment or in the car when you don't have a book or pen and paper handy.
